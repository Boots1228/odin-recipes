Pad Thai

The Most Well-Known Thai Dish

What does REAL traditional pad thai look like?


First, it should not have ketchup or any tomato product in it. Second, it should not be syrupy sweet. And finally, it shouldn't be just a heavy, soggy clump of noodles with bits of green onions.

Ingredients

  • Pad Thai Noodles 1.00 fold
  • Exotic Pad Thai Sauce 4.00tbsp
  • Prawns 3.00 pcs
  • Beans Sprouts 0.50 pc
  • Vegetable Oil 3.00 tbsp
  • Garlic chopped 3.00 cloves
  • Water 0.25 cup
  • Dried Shrimp 3.00 tbsp
  • Chives 10.00 pcs
  • Pan Rosted Peanut 0.25 cup
  • Water for boiling noodles

Steps

  1. Bring your water to a boil in a sauce pan or pot. Add your Pad Thai Noodles(rice noodles) to the boiling water for four minutes, stirring occassionally and strain.
  2. Add vegetable oil, and add the prawns or shrimps and garlic to the heated pan.
  3. Remove the prawns and set aside, add the strained noodles.Back into the pan and add the Pad Thai Sauce.
  4. Give it a good stir and add the bean sprouts. Remove from heat and add chives.
  5. Serve the noodles with prawns on top, a lime to squeeze.
